Our People

Owner Yuri Pavlov

Yuri grew up in New York and was always interested in the culinary arts. With both of his parents being from Russia, his intial specialties being Russian cuisine. It didn't take long for Yuri to venture out into some New York specialties, where he found an intrest in pizza. Over the years, Yuri perfected the New York slice, ventured into the Chicago Deep Dish; and even did a few of his on takes on pizza fusions. Yuri's pizza adventure would lead him to meet his wife at a food festival, which eventually resulted in the founding of Pizza Corp 2010.

Owner Ji-ah Cho

Ji-ah has always been in the New York food scene, with her family owning several resturaunts. She planned on opening her own resturant one day , but wasn't planning on it being pizza. During a summer food festival where she was promoting the family resturants with her family, she met Yuri. They married and Yuri worked at one of her families resturants , until one day they decided that Pizza Corp would be a good addition to the family resturants; and founded Pizza Corp in 2010.

Dessert Chef Tonya Jackson

Tonya grew up in Brooklyn, and enjoyed baking desserts as a kid with her mom and grandma. When she graduated highschool, she decided that she wanted to make baking her full time occupation; and get her degree from the Culinary Institute of America. Tonya met Ji-ah at a event where she was catering the desserts. They became friends and Tonya catered some of the corporate events for Ji-ah's family resturants. Tonya was asked to come on full time at Pizza Corp to create the dessert menu, and train the other dessert chefs for all the locations; and make desserts in the Manhattan location.

Chef Thomas Fischer

Thomas Fischer grew up in Hamburg, and after school had an opportunity to work at the Landhaus Scherrer. Eventually he decided to come to America to help with his Uncle's resturant. After gaining an excellent reputation for turning out some of the best steak, chicken and seafood dishes in New York; he was asked to join Pizza Corp. He is in charge of the non-pizza part of the menu, and training the other chefs at all the Pizza Corp locations.

Catering Chefs John and Han

John and Han are a dynamic duo know as the "Catering Boyz", their friendship started in Culinary school, and they ventured out with a catering business. They are well known for delivering quality food and friendly service. They now manage all the Pizza Corp catering services. From menu planning , training , to assisting with event planning. They still like to get out in the field , so you might catch them at an event.
